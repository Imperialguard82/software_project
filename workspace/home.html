Welcome to the 4th Year Project Portal

This portal is designed to fit help automate the process of assigning 4th years projects.
This is the home page designed to link to a number of pages although what it links to depends on who is logging in
but for the purpose of this prototype you will see all the pages that wil exist.
The Projects for Students is meant for students to look at to see the details of their project.
The Projects for Supervisors is designed to allow supervisors to see the projects they are supervising.
The Supervisors page is designed to do different things depending on who has logged in, for the administrator
it allows the administrator to create the supervisors list. For students and Lecturers it allows them to see the list of supervisors.
The Examiners page is designed to create a list of examiners for each project for a lecturer to look at.
The Add Projects page is designed to allow studnets or administrators to add their project ideas.
The Pick Proects page allows students to pick their preferred project.
The Check Projects page allows an Administrator to check that a lecturers idea is valid.
